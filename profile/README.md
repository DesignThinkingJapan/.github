We're a Tokyo studio that pairs human-centered design with AI engineering — from problem discovery to working solution in weeks, not quarters.

**What we do**

- **AI Leadership Academy** — Executive program. Two days. Twelve leaders. One cohort at a time.
- **Build** — Custom AI engineering. 12 weeks from first conversation to production.
- **Future Signals** — Annual research on where AI is actually heading.

**The team that discovers the problem builds the solution.** Every person on an engagement holds dual capability — design methodology and technical implementation. No handoffs.
